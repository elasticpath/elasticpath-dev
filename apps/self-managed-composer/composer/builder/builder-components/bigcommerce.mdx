## Connections

### BigCommerce OAuth 2.0

1. To create an OAuth 2.0 app in BigCommerce, sign up for a BigCommerce developer account at https://developer.bigcommerce.com/ and create a new BigCommerce application.
2. Take note of your application's Client ID and Secret and enter those values in Composer when you add a BigCommerce connection to your integration.
3. Under Redirect URI, add Composer's callback URL, https://oauth2.us-east-2.elasticpathintegrations.com/callback.

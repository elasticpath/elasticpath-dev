You can localize product names and descriptions when creating your products and hierarchies. Localizing your products and hierarchies enables you to expand your reach to diverse markets. Adapting your product names and descriptions to meet linguistic requirements improves customer satisfaction and enhances brand loyalty.

You can do this when creating your products and hierarchies.

- using the API. See [Create a product](/docs/api/pxm/products/create-product) and [Create a hierarchy](/docs/api/pxm/products/create-hierarchy).
- using Commerce Manager. See [Using Locales](/docs/commerce-manager/product-experience-manager/locales/).

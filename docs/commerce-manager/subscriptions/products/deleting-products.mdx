---
title: Deleting Repeat Products
nav_label: Deleting Repeat Products
sidebar_position: 30
---

You cannot delete a product that is part of an offering. You must remove a product from an offering before deleting it.

1. Log in to Commerce Manager.
2. Go to **SUBSCRIPTIONS** > **Repeat Products**.
3. Either:

    - Select **...** on a product that you want to remove, and then select **Delete**.
    - Select the checkbox on any product that you want to remove and then select **Actions** > **Delete**.

4. Select **Delete** to delete the product and close the confirmation dialog.

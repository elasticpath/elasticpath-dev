---
title: Adding Image to Article in Blog
nav_label: Adding Image to Article in Blog
sidebar_position: 50
---

1. Create a new line by clicking the ```<Enter>``` key
2. Once you're on a new, empty line you'll see a greyed plus icon to the right of your cursor
3. Click the greyed plus icon to expand a tray of options:

   Out of this tray, you'll select the first option which is an image icon.

   ![Image icon](/assets/studio/image-icon.png)

4. Once selected the Media Modal will open allowing you to select an already existing image or upload a new one.
5. After you select an image click "Save" to add it to your blog post.

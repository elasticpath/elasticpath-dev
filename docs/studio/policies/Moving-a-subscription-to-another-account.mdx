title: Moving Subscription to Another Account
nav_label: Moving Subscription to Another Account
sidebar_position: 30
---

At this time, it's ***not currently possible*** to transfer a paid subscription between two (or more) Studio accounts.
That said, the same end result can be achieved by following the below steps:

1. Cancel the paid plan of one of your accounts by requesting a
   cancellation [via Elastic Path Support.](https://support.elasticpath.com/hc/en-us/)
2. Log into the account you'd like to Upgrade and navigate to **settings**.
3. From here, select **Upgrade** and complete the in-app upgrade flow.

Follow these steps below to connect a domain hosted on **OVH** to your Studio
account. [Here's is a link to their official documentation.](https://docs.ovh.com/us/en/domains/web_hosting_how_to_edit_my_dns_zone/).

1. Log into your OVH account and navigate to your OVH Control Panel.
2. Click on the domains dropdown on the left hand navigation bar and click "All my domains".
3. Click on the domain you are wanting to connect to Studio and click on "DNS zone" which is the second option in the
   overhead navigation bar.
4. Click on the "Add an entry" button on the far right of the DNS zone page and select the type of record you are
   adding (A, CNAME, www).
5. In the "Sub-domain" text field, type in the type of record (A, CNAME, www) and in the "Target" text field paste in
   the URL that associates with the record found under the "value" column in Studio. Click "next" to save the record.

:::note
Keep in mind that it may take up to 48 hours for Studio to reflect the changes you've made in OVH.
:::

Follow the below steps to connect a domain hosted on **Dreamhost** to your Studio
account. [Here is a link to their official documentation.](https://help.dreamhost.com/hc/en-us/articles/360035516812-Adding-custom-DNS-records)

1. Log into your **Dreamhost** account and navigate to your DNS settings.

    * If in [Grid view](https://help.dreamhost.com/hc/en-us/articles/360043889332-Using-the-new-Manage-Websites-page),
      click the 'vertical 3 dots' button under your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.
    * If in [List view](https://help.dreamhost.com/hc/en-us/articles/360043889332-Using-the-new-Manage-Websites-page),
      click the 'vertical 3 dots' button to the right of your domain and then click the DNS Settings link.

2. Click the "Add Record" button, hover over the A Record or the CNAME section and click the "ADD" link. Click the Add
   Record button to save it.

:::note
Keep in mind that it may take up to 48 hours for Studio to reflect the changes you've made in Dreamhost.
:::

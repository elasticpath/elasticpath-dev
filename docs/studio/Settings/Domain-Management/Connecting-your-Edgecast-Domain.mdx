Follow the below steps to connect a domain hosted on **Edgecast** to your Studio account. Here is a link to their
[official documentation](https://docs.edgecast.com/pdfs/VDMS_Route_Admin_Guide.pdf)

1. Navigate to the Route (DNS) page.
2. Click on the desired zone and select "Add Record".
3. Add record information from Studio settings tab under the domain section (A record, CNAME).
4. Click "Add" and click "Submit Group" to save your changes.

:::note
Keep in mind that it may take up to 48 hours for Studio to reflect the changes you've made in Edgecast.
:::

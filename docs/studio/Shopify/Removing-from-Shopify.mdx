In order to remove Studio from your Shopify account you need to remove the Studio Sales channel. This is done by
following the below steps which have been taken
from [this Shopify Guide](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-sales-channels).

1. From your Shopify admin, click **Settings**, and then click **Sales channels**.
2. Click **Remove** beside **Studio**.
3. Provide a reason for uninstalling.
4. Click to confirm.
5. The Studio Sales channel should now be removed from your Shopify store.
